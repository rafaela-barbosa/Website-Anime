    • Sinopse

    The Japanese action anime Demon Slayer (Kimetsu No Yaiba) premiered on April 6, 2019, and is produced by the studio Ufotable. It quickly became popular all over the world. The story revolves around Tanjiro Kamado, who goes on a journey to save his sister Nezuko, who was attacked by demons.
    In Kimetsu no Yaiba, Tanjiro Kamado, a kind and dedicated young man who supports his family by selling charcoal, sees his life turned upside down when he finds his family brutally murdered by a demon. As if the tragedy were not enough, his younger sister, Nezuko, the only survivor, has been transformed into something he could never have imagined: a demon. Determined to find a cure for his sister and avenge his family, Tanjiro embarks on a dangerous and thrilling journey, facing dark creatures, challenging his own limits and discovering that, even in the midst of darkness, hope can shine like the flame of a sword.

    Tanjiro Kamado

    Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonistof Demon Slayer. He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado, back into a human and to hunt down and kill demons, and later swore to defeat Muzan Kibutsuji, the King of Demons, in order to prevent others from suffering the same fate as him.

    Nezuko Kamado

    Nezuko Kamado is the deuteragonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. She is a demon and the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the two remaining members of the Kamado family. Formerly a human, she was attacked and transformed into a demon by Muzan Kibutsuji.

    Muzan Kibutsuji

    Muzan Kibutsuji is the main antagonist of Demon Slayer. He is the Demon King, the progenitor of all other demons in existence, and the leader of the Twelve Kizuki, an organization made up of the twelve strongest demons under his direct command.
